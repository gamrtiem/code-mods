go to config, PrideFlags folder, template.xcf

paste in your flag, recommend https://www.flagcolorcodes.com and scale it appropriately (if you have little experience in gimp, holding shift while resizing will allow you to resize it without respecting it's aspect ratio)

once properly scaled right click on one of the layer masks of the layers below and hit "Masks to selection", your flag should now look like this 

now right click your new flag and hit "Add Layer Masks...", then select "Selection". 

your flag should now be ready to export
